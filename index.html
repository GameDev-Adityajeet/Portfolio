Unreal Engine 5BlueprintsEnemy AIAction-Adventure RPG

Overview

An action-adventure RPG inspired by Hindu mythology, built in Unreal Engine 5. Players take on the role of Indra fighting the demon Vritrasur across immersive 3D environments with a dynamic combat system and narrative-rich progression.

Key Features

  • Custom combat system with combo chains and ability cooldowns
  • Enemy AI with patrol, alert, and attack states using Behaviour Trees
  • Designed and balanced full narrative arc with branching dialogue
  • 3D immersive environments with atmospheric lighting in UE5
  • Won 1st & 3rd Prize at CU Project Expo 2025
