Overview

Indra vs Vritra is a narration-based adventure game inspired by the legendary battle between Indra, the king of gods, and the demon Vritrasur as told in the Shrimad Bhagwat Mahapuran. Built in Unity using C#, this project served as the initial prototype that would later evolve into the full-scale Legend of Indra vs Vritrasur on Unreal Engine 5.

I contributed to player controls, environment design, storyboarding, and served as the Game's Director — overseeing the creative vision and narrative direction of the project. Character animations were driven by Mixamo, providing fluid and expressive movement throughout the gameplay experience.

This prototype was instrumental in exploring the mythology-based narrative structure, testing gameplay mechanics, and validating the overall design direction before committing to the larger Unreal Engine production.
