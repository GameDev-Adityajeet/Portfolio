The Shooter

A polished, feature-rich first-person shooter built in Unity. Advanced player movement, a modular multi-weapon system, decal-based impact effects, and refined enemy AI all come together for a satisfying FPS experience.

Overview

The Shooter is a fully-featured, Unity-based first-person shooter developed between February and May 2025. The project was an exercise in building a AAA-feel FPS experience from scratch — focusing on responsiveness, visual feedback, and satisfying gameplay loops.

As the sole designer and developer, I built the entire weapon system from scratch using a modular architecture that allows easy addition of new weapons. The character controller features advanced movement mechanics including sprinting, crouching, and smooth camera bob.

The enemy AI was tuned across multiple playtests to ensure fair but challenging encounters, with clear audio-visual feedback on each hit and kill.

Key Features

  • Modular multi-weapon system — each weapon has unique fire rate, damage, recoil, and reload animations
  • Decal-based bullet impact FX on all surface types including wood, metal, and character bodies
  • Advanced character controller with sprint, crouch, smooth camera bob, and head-tilt on movement
  • Enemy AI with awareness states — idle patrol, alert tracking, and attack — tuned for balanced difficulty
  • State-machine driven animation system for smooth transitions between weapon states and movement
  • Performance profiled and optimised to maintain a stable 60fps on mid-range hardware
